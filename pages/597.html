Type : PLACEMENT
Subject : Urgent
Company : CDC Placement process 2025-26

All the MS and PhD students (except sponsorship students) who are willing to register for placement session 2025-26 are advised to take a note of the followings.

a) They should write an application (hardcopy) addressed to Chairperson CDC that he / she will submit the synopsis latest by 31st January 2026, and once the defense is done he / she will be out of the placement process. It is the responsibility of the student to inform CDC about this event.

b) They should also declare that they have not registered with CDC in the session 2024-25.

c) This aforesaid application should be approved by the supervisor before sending it to CDC.

d) CDC after verifying the same can give access to the student for 3/ 4 days and within that time period the student has to prepare his / her CV in ERP (CDC tab).

e) Hence all the students (PhD / MS) applying for placement registration at CDC, is advised to check their email IDs at regular intervals.

f) Once the registration process is complete, the students can see what companies (In CDC Notice Board, ERP-CDC tab) are coming and they can apply in those companies only which are open for their department and degree.
