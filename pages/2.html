Subject : Urgent
Company : Travelling window for potentially eligible students for Internship 2025-26

All the students who will register for CDC internship process of 2025-26 may note:

Travel window for the potentially eligible students for internship will be from 11th July to 13th July, 2025.

All the tests (if conducted) before the travel window time will be conducted online, that means students can write their tests / process from their respective locations.

From 18th July 2025 onwards all the tests may be conducted at Nalanda and student must come to Nalanda to write the test / process, otherwise their candidature will be treated as null and void.
