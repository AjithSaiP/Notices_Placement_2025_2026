Company : CDC Placement Process_2025-26

CDC will start the ERP registration for placement 2025-26 from 12th August 2025 (after 7:00 PM onwards).

Students have to complete their placement registration process (CV building) within 25th August 2025 (11:59 PM). No further extension will be provided.

All the potential eligible students will be provided two consecutive registration windows i.e. one in the month of August, 2025 (for first phase till December) and another in the month of January, 2026 (for second phase till March). By registering in the month of August they can participate in Phase - I placement session i.e in December and by registering in the month of January they can participate in Phase - II placement i.e. from January to March. It is the choice of the students on which phase (Phase - I in December and Phase - II from January to March) they want to register.

However, once a student is graduated from the Institute he/she will not be permitted to participate in any further placement process, even though he/ she has not availed two opportunities or availed but not placed.

All the students who have accepted the PPO are strictly advised to register for CDC placement, otherwise in CDC placement data their name will be missed out.

Students are advised to keep their CV content ready along with the relevant documents which are needed to be uploaded for further verification.
