Job Description: Description: Caprae Capital Partners – PE Internship Program
Caprae Capital Partners is a global investment platform backed by a U.S. family office. Unlike traditional private equity funds, we are a technology-enabled business designed to modernize and disrupt the investment industry. We partner with entrepreneurs, operators, and founders while directly building long-term businesses within our ecosystem.
Internship Overview
This program is designed for exceptional students who aspire to build careers in private equity, investment banking, or management consulting. As a Buyside Research & Financial Due Diligence Intern, you will work closely with our U.S. and India teams on live transactions.
Your responsibilities will include:
• Conducting detailed industry and company research to identify acquisition opportunities.
• Performing financial statement analysis, valuation, and modeling to assess potential investments.
• Supporting the due diligence process, including market mapping, competitor benchmarking, and risk analysis.
• Preparing investment memoranda, research reports, and presentation materials for senior leadership.
• Participating in weekly deal review discussions with global deal teams.
• Collaborating with advisers, brokers, and in-house platforms to advance transactions.
Key Highlights
• Workload: 60+ hours per week (full immersion in live projects).
• Exposure: Direct experience with U.S. and international M&A transactions.
• Mentorship: Guidance from senior professionals in private equity and corporate development.
• Skill Development: Hands-on training in financial modeling, valuation, due diligence, and strategic analysis.
• Career Outcomes: Strong foundation for roles in private equity, consulting, and investment banking.
Candidate Profile
• Students from IIT Madras, IIT Bombay, and other leading institutions pursuing degrees in Engineering, Economics, Finance, or Management.
• Strong analytical ability and attention to detail.
• Excellent written and spoken English.
• Capacity to commit significant time and effort in a high-performance environment.
• Intellectual curiosity and a desire to learn how global investments are executed.
Future Opportunities
High-performing interns may be invited to continue with Caprae Capital in advanced roles, including deal sourcing, investment analysis, and portfolio strategy. Alumni of our program have gone on to pursue careers in top global firms and entrepreneurial ventures.
Our Hiring Process
Our hiring process is designed to identify candidates who align with both our professional standards and long-term vision. The process begins with applicants submitting their resume and completing a virtual, pre-recorded interview. Selected candidates will then be asked to complete a written submission that outlines their career aspirations and demonstrates their understanding of our investment criteria. Submissions are carefully reviewed and scored, and candidates who achieve a high enough evaluation will be invited to participate in a live Zoom interview with one of our company’s founders. This structured approach ensures we assess both technical and cultural fit at each stage of the process.
