About Intech Organics Limited
Intech Organics Limited is a leading chemical manufacturing organization engaged in the
production of bromine and bromine-based compounds, inorganic chemicals, and specialty
chemicals. With a strong commitment to innovation, sustainability, and operational
excellence, we are continuously expanding our manufacturing capabilities and global
presence.
We believe in nurturing young engineering talent and providing them with opportunities to
learn, grow, and contribute to our vision of chemical excellence.
Position: Graduate Engineer Trainee (GET)
Job Purpose
The Graduate Engineer Trainee program aims to develop young engineering professionals
into future leaders in manufacturing operations, maintenance, projects, and process
optimization. Selected candidates will undergo structured training at our manufacturing
facility, gaining hands-on exposure to plant operations, safety, quality, and continuous
improvement initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
 Assist in day-to-day plant operations, monitoring key process parameters, and
ensuring smooth production activities.
 Support preventive and predictive maintenance activities to enhance equipment
reliability and uptime.
 Participate in troubleshooting and root cause analysis for process and equipmentrelated issues.
 Work on improvement projects related to productivity, energy efficiency, and process
optimization.
 Ensure compliance with safety, health, and environmental regulations.
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams in quality assurance, production planning,
and engineering.
 Prepare technical reports, documentation, and standard operating procedures
(SOPs).
Educational Qualifications
 B.Tech / B.E. in Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or Instrumentation
& Electrical Engineering
 Graduating Batch: 2026 (from IITs)
Desired Skills & Attributes
 Strong academic background with sound technical fundamentals.
 Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
 Positive attitude and willingness to learn.
 Ability to work effectively in a plant environment and in teams.
 Strong commitment to safety and sustainability practices.
Compensation & Benefits
 Compensation will be as per industry standards and commensurate with
qualifications and performance.
 Additional benefits include accommodation assistance, canteen facilities, and
medical coverage as per company policy.
Selection Process
1. Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) – Introduction to Intech Organics Limited and career
prospects.
2. Aptitude Test
3. Technical Test
4. Technical Interview
5. HR Interview
Career Progression
Upon successful completion of the training period, GETs will be confirmed as Assistant
Engineers or equivalent roles, based on performance and organizational requirements.
